When you have issues or service disruptions, knowing how to report a problem can help. This guide gives steps to make sure your concerns are sorted out quickly and properly.

Steps for reporting a problem

  1. Collect Information:

    • Gather all important details about the issue, like dates, times, and any past chats or reference numbers.

  2. First Contact:

    • Get in touch with the service provider's customer service team. Use their official ways to contact them, like phone, email, or online portals.

  3. Check Back:

    • If the issue is not sorted, check back with the customer service team. Get a reference number of all chats for future use.

  4. Take It Higher - escalate to your Ward Councilor:

    • If the issue continues, ask to speak with a supervisor or manager. Clearly explain the issue and provide all needed information.
