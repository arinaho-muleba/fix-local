# How to report PARKS and RECREATION issues in Joburg

![Placeholder Image](https://storageforfixlocal.blob.core.windows.net/dev/content/d89d6623-6c7f-4f1b-9586-75c4f415c1a1/images/d89d6623-6c7f-4f1b-9586-75c4f415c1a1.webp)

To make sure our city services work well, it's important to report issues like potholes, power cuts, water leaks, or fallen trees. Following up and taking more steps if needed can help fix these issues. Here’s how you can report these issues in Joburg.

Please read point 4 for reporting issues with PARKS and RECREATION.

## Steps for reporting issues: 


1. **Go to the Joburg eservices website to log a fault: **  
- [City of Joburg e-services](https://eservices.joburg.org.za/pages/Reportacityproblem.aspx)


2. **Call the Joburg Call Centre:**
- Phone: 0860 56 28 74
- Email: [joburgconnect@joburg.org.za](joburgconnect@joburg.org.za)


3. **Register on Log Now Smart City:**
- Sign up at [Log Now](https://www.lognow.co.za/) to report city problems and see FAQs.


4. **Report PARKS and RECREATION issues:**
