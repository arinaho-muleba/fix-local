How to report SAFETY and SECURITY issues in Joburg

To make sure our city services work well, it's important to report issues like potholes, power cuts, water leaks, or fallen trees. Following up and taking more steps if needed can help fix these issues. Hereï¿½s how you can report these issues in Joburg.

Steps for reporting issues

  1. Go to the Joburg eservices website to log a fault:
  • City of Joburg e-services
  1. Call the Joburg Call Centre:
  • Phone: 0860 56 28 74
  • Email: joburgconnect@joburg.org.za

  • Sign up at Log Now to report city problems and see FAQs.
